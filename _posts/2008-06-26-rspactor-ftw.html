Since I’m the business of pimping out other people’s stuff today, I’d like to point you over to RSpactor. It’s an autotest-like that takes all the goodness but doesn’t have the problems that autotest has. The two things that jumped out at me:

  • Very fast, and doesn’t eat up any CPU cycles or memory
  • Automatically had a slick growl notification configured, once I installed growlnotify.sh

Check it out. I wish I had seen it earlier (okay, what I really mean is I wish I had had a spec suite that ran fast enough to warrant RSpactor). Note that it only runs on OS X.5, because it makes clever use of OS X’s filesystem events.
