My twitter plugin didn’t really get any responses, except for one short thread on the rails mailing list.

So I just wanted to say that this was just a joke plugin. My friend asked me if I knew what Twitter was, I said no, he showed me, I thought it was the dumbest thing in the world. So I spent 15 minutes writing a plugin that posted to Twitter whenever a record was saved.
