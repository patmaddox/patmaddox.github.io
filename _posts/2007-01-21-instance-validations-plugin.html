This is the little announcement page for my new instance validations plugin. It’s an incredibly ugly monkey patch that allows you to specify validations on individual AR objects. It’s basically just an enabler plugin for my workflow engine. Maybe someone else will find it useful.

I almost called it acts_as_individually_validatable, but that’s just stupid (scroll to the middle or cmd+f ‘stupid’)
