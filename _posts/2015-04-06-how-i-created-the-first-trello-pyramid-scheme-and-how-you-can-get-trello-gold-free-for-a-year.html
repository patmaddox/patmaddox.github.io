Last week I ran a 48-hour product challenge - and I shipped a product!

I had a ton of fun along the way... and created the first-ever Trello pyramid scheme :) Trello gives you one free month of Gold access for every person you refer... up to 12 people. Which means there's no point in me continuining to refer people under my own link. Instead, I'm switching out my referral link for the next person in the list, ensuring that we all get Trello Gold for a year, for FREE!

I <3 Trello... I use it all the time, and you can see on the challenge page that I used it to keep track of things while I created RubySteps: The Playbook. If you want to get Trello Gold free for a year, sign up for my Trello-approved Trello Gold pyramid scheme!
